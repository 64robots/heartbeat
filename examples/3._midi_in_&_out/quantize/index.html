First record some events with your midi keyboard. After you have stopped the recording, the recorded events will be printed to the log on the left side. In the log on the right side appear the events of the song. Initially, the logs will display exactly the same information. If you click the "quantize" button the log on the right side will be updated and show the quantized events.

In this example you can only quantize to quarter notes, but heartbeat allows you to quantize events to arbitrary values, see the documentation.
